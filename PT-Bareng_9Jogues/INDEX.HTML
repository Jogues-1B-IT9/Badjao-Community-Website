Introduction

The Badjao also known as the Sea Gypsis/Sea normads a community of people living along the shores of Mindanao, Borneo, and etc... They promote different livehood programs for the community and trains them into developing and preserving their culture. They are facing challenges of lack of basic needs espically medience and food. it is also said that because of the lockdown many of them are having a hard time with their buisnesses many sold it just to buy food. And not only that students in the Badjao community are in need of gadgets for their education.

Information

Insitution name:
Badjao community Association of Matina Aplaya Inc.

Mission:
The association was established with the purpose of becoming
a model among the populace
of the badjao community.

History:
Badjaos have been spotted asking for alms along the city’s busy streets
Some of the Badjao have started to form communities
along the shores. Due to these problems the institution
was formed to provide a better quality of life for the people.
The Badjao Community Association
of Matina Aplaya Inc. started with the help of pastor
Felicisimo Morales and his wife in Isla Verde

Beneficiaries
The beneficiaries are the Badjao
community which are also known as
“Sea Gypsies” or Sea Nomads”.
They are an ethnic group
found along the shores of Mindanao,
Borneo, Celebes and Indonesia.

Benefactors:
They have formed a partnership ADDU JHS to help resolve these problems.
